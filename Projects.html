Oh God it's a Slime

I worked on this project with Daniel Gealow. In this java project my partner and me designed an ASCII based dungeon crawler where you controlled a player who tried to outwit and defeat slimes within a dungeon. I implemented a key listener, JFrame GUI, and designed an ASCII map reader function. I also gained some very basic experience with understanding Creative Commons licenses and attribution.
